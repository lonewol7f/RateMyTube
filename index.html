Privacy Policy for RateMyTube Chrome Extension

Effective Date: 11/1/2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to RateMyTube, a Chrome extension developed by Team OPAC. This Privacy Policy is intended to inform you about the types of data we collect when you use our Chrome extension, how we use that data, and your choices concerning your information. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information.

2. Data Collection

2.1. Information We Collect

When you use the RateMyTube Chrome extension, we may collect the following types of information:

  • 2.1.1. Active YouTube Video URLs: RateMyTube accesses the active tab's URL to identify YouTube video content. We collect the URLs of the YouTube videos you are watching for the purpose of generating ratings and scores.
  • 2.1.2. Extension Usage Information: We may collect information related to your use of the extension, such as your interaction with the RateMyTube interface and the generated scores.

2.2. Usage of Collected Information

The data we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • 2.2.1. Generating Ratings and Scores: We use the YouTube video URLs to generate ratings and scores for the content you are watching.
  • 2.2.2. Improving the Extension: We may use aggregated and anonymized data to enhance and improve the RateMyTube extension.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • 3.1. Legal Obligations: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with legal obligations or to protect and defend the rights or property of Team OPAC.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Choices

5.1. Access and Correction

You can request to access or correct the information we have collected from you by contacting us at team.opac23@gmail.com.

5.2. Data Deletion

You can request the deletion of your data by contacting us at team.opac23@gmail.com. Please note that certain data may be retained for legal or legitimate business purposes.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy
